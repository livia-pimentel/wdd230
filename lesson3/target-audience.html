Scenarios

How did the entrepreneurs work together?

Through meetings with the Camaçari Chamer of Commerce team that will bring ideas for improvement actions in the sector, also listening to the opinion and ideas of the associates. Thus making a strategic plan of action.

How will the search for partnership with the public power be like?

Through petitions the secretariat wishes and also possible meetings with local politicians.

Why would the government accept a partnership with the private sector, which seeks profits?

Because the increase in revenue for the company reflects in more income for the government, through taxes and contributions, not to mention the social part that is the generation of jobs.

What resources will be made available to members to improve their business?

Courses and lectures done with a specialized team.

In relation to the governmental doubts that may exist, in which will we be given support?

Support will be provided for questions regarding inspection and fees applied to the sector (remembering that the fee is neither a tax nor a contribution).
