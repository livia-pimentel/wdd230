From our family to yours!

Bountiful Foods is a small family business that began in the 1990s with Joseph Y. White, a visionary who at the time was already concerned with the quality of food for his family, so he decided to produce his own food for that purpose. guarantee its quality and food safety. Gradually, the whole family was involved in the project and began to actively participate in its cultivation, and what initially was to serve only his family has expanded.
And over the years, production has become increasingly professional, acquiring USDA NOP (National Organic Program) certifications to provide our customers with safety and quality.
We are located in Carlsbad, California, however, and have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego.
Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made-to-order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas.
With the expansion of the business, it became necessary to partner with small rural producers, to have greater diversity and greater reach. The same selectivity that exists at Bountiful Foods is passed on to our partners, in addition to monitoring and demanding quality seals that attest that the product is 100% organic and free of pesticides.
